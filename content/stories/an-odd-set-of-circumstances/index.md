This is a tale about Aegis. He's a young adult with many different friends he hangs out with from time to time. However, none of those bonds could compare to the one he has with Ollie. Ollie has many of the same interests and hobbies Aegis has, and they do a lot of activities with each other.

At home, he kept one interest he had away from anyone else. He loved the concept of fictional bubbles. Bubbles of all shapes, sizes, and forms. He'd fantasize about them night after night, wishing to feel the soap against him,

Aegis and Ollie were riding a subway train when one asks about a bet they had placed yesterday. They bet on whether a stock would go up or down after a day. Aegis bet down, Ollie bet up. Both men were nervous as they checked the stock's prices. "It's up," Ollie said with excitement. Aegis gave a slight groan at the thought of what Ollie would make him do for losing a bet.

Ollie was searching around the train for inspiration on what Aegis' punishment should be. After seeing a soda can with small bubbles on the sides, he knew exactly what to say. "How would you feel about drinking a whole bottle of bubble solution," Ollie said with a smirk. Although Aegis liked bubbles, he most certainly did not like the taste of soap. Yet, he had no choice in the matter. A bet's a bet, and he already agreed to anything.

Once the pair were off the subway, they looked around for a store that would sell bubble solution, one very reluctantly. After a few minutes of looking, they spotted a party store to the left. They went in, found what they were looking for, paid for their purchase, and walked out.

Sitting at the curb, Aegis took the bottle and drank it in one fell swoop. He gave a few lemon faces when drinking it, but was done in less than a minute. Ollie was impressed with the speed and satisfied that his dare was complete. After such a daring move, the two decided it was time to part for the day and go home. They were starting to get tired themselves and agreed that it was time to go.

As he rode home, he let other things take his mind, like social media or how empty this train was. However, he still had the aftertaste of soap in his mouth. He somewhat worried about what could happen as a side effect and dropped down into his own bubbly fantasies. Around thirty minutes later, he reached his apartment, got himself ready for bed, and went to sleep.

He woke up to an especially bright sun. It wasn't too bright, but it was different enough to notice. Your eyes could've just been watery, or maybe the window blinds were open? Any way it was, he felt quite comfy in bed, and since it was an off day, he could spare the time to lay down a little longer.

Eventually, it comes time where he has to actually get out of bed and get ready for the day ahead. He sat up, still somewhat groggy, and started to get on his feet. However, his feet felt something strange. Instead of the wood flooring he was used to in his room, the floor was instead more glossy and smooth. He looked down and found that the ground was lighter than usual and seemed to change colors.

As he came to his senses, he noticed that the reason for the brighter sun, the lighter floor, and glossy texture was all around him. He was in a giant bubble! As soon as he found out, he was instantly happier. This was all of his dreams come true, he could finally feel what it was like to be in a bubble!

He went to his phone to tell Ollie the news. He tried reaching through the bubble to grab his phone, but he found that the bubble stretched with his hand. As he pushed harder, the bubble grew stronger and stronger before he couldn't exert anymore and the bubble rebounded into a spherical shape.

He bent down and grabbed the phone and tried to pull it in, but it slipped out before it would go through the surface. He tried a few more times, the phone slipping out of his membrane-wrapped hands each time. In the end, he settles for just resting it on the bed and tapping it from up close.
